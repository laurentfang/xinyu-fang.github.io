## About Me

I'm a PhD roboticist ([Cornell](https://www.cornell.edu/) · [Ferrari Lab](http://lisc.mae.cornell.edu/))
building end-to-end **AI, machine learning, and robotics systems** — dynamics modeling, nonlinear
control, perception, simulation, and the iOS apps that ship them.

I am currently an **Autonomous UAS Engineer at Buzz Inc.**,
where I lead the development of an AI-powered, iOS-based emergency-dispatch platform that mobilizes
certified drone pilots for fire departments, law-enforcement agencies, and FEMA in real time. I
completed my PhD in December 2025 under the advisement of
Prof. [Silvia Ferrari](https://www.mae.cornell.edu/faculty-directory/silvia-ferrari) on
**bio-inspired neuromorphic control and full flight-envelope identification for flapping-wing micro
aerial vehicles**.

Across four peer-reviewed publications (including *Nature Communications*) and industry work at
**Buzz Inc.**, **Eve Vehicles**, and **Ferrari S.p.A.**, my work translates ideas from animal flight
control and modern ML into deployable autonomy for real platforms — from insect-scale flyers to
BVLOS drone operations and ADAS.

Prior to Cornell, I earned my B.S. in Chemical Engineering (minor in Physics) at
[The Ohio State University](https://www.osu.edu/), advised by Prof. [Xiaoguang Wang](https://cbe.osu.edu/people/wang.12206).

Outside work, I'm a father of a Samoyed (Coconut) and 8 cats (Issac, Burberry, Jona, Humphrey, Blink,
Charlotte, Tisha, and Lily). I enjoy driving, off-roading, camping, kayaking, and hiking.
